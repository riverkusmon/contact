Program Name & Description

The Andrew Gardner SMS program is a conversational, two-way text messaging service. When you submit our contact form and agree to receive texts, or when you text our number first, you agree to receive SMS replies from us related to your inquiry — including scheduling, confirmations, follow-ups, and answers to your questions.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies and depends on your interactions with us. You may receive recurring messages in response to the messages you send.

Cost

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Andrew Gardner does not charge for the messages themselves.

How to Get Help

For help, reply HELP at any time, or email riverkusmon@gmail.com.

How to Opt Out

You can cancel at any time by replying STOP. After you reply STOP, you will receive one final message confirming that you have been unsubscribed, and you will not receive any further messages unless you text us again or sign up again.

Opt-out keywords: STOP, STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT  ·  Help keywords: HELP, INFO
