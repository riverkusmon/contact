Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Andrew Gardner (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website and SMS text messaging program (the “Service”).

Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form or text us, we collect the information you provide — your name, email address, mobile phone number, and the content of your messages. We use this information only to respond to you.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule and confirm appointments, answer questions, and send the follow-up messages you request. We do not use your information for any unrelated purpose.

SMS / Text Messaging

By providing your mobile number and checking the consent box on our form (or by texting us first), you agree to receive SMS text messages from us related to your inquiry. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information, and text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties.

Sharing of Information

Information may be shared only with the service providers that help us operate the Service (such as our SMS provider, Twilio), and only to the extent needed to deliver messages to you. We will never share your information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Data Retention & Security

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service and meet our legal obligations, and we use reasonable measures to protect it.

Your Choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. To request that we delete your information, contact us at the email below.
